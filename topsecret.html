RSVP - Please let us know before March 1, 2019

But really just tell 'YES!', right now. Because of course you'll join us!

When: The marriage ceremony will take place at 4:30-5:30pm on Saturday, August 10th, with dinner to follow.
However, we want you to join for the whole weekend! That is Friday 4:30pm to Sunday midday.

Location: Casco, Maine (see invitaion for address)

Transportation: Casco, ME is about a 2.5-3 hour drive from Boston. To reduce the amount of cars, we strongly encourage you all to carpool together. If you have any issues or need to find someone to carpool with, please contact us, as we can help coordinate drivers. Please don’t forget to indicate your driving preferences when you RSVP.

Recommended Attire and Packing List:
  • Friday night - casual attire, maybe light sweater if it gets cold
  • Saturday morning/afternoon - casual attire, workout clothes, swimming gear
  • Wedding Ceremony - dressy casual (don’t wear sharp heels as they will stick in the grass; make sure you are comfortable for dancing and sitting on picnic blankets; the lake can get breezy so you might want a shawl or sweater)
  • Sunday brunch - casual attire
  • Please Bring: warm clothes for nights, swimwear and coverups for day, athletic clothes for playing, rain gear in case, makeup, hair accessories or products, sunglasses, any instruments you want to play by the fire!
Sleeping Arrangements: *Once everyone RSVPs, we will notify you of the sleeping arrangements!

If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
Thank you and we hope to celebrate this special weekend with you!
